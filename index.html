    <section id="introduction">

        <h2>Introduction and Background</h2>

        <p>This project was designed to study and find out which factors influence living standards in cities. Based on this, we are going to provide students, young professionals, and city stakeholders with valuable insights into how corporate and city policies impact urban sustainability.</p>

    </section>


    <section id="goal">

        <h2>Project Goal</h2>

        <p>The goal of this project is to analyze and disseminate information on urban sustainability to inform students, young professionals, businesses, urban planners, and citizens to make informed decisions and advocate for sustainable urban development. The project aims to foster more livable, greener, and economically thriving cities worldwide by offering key insights for students' careers, guiding businesses towards responsible practices, recommending strategies for sustainable urban development, and assisting city dwellers in advocating for positive changes.</p>

    </section>


    <section id="description">

        <h2>Project Description</h2>

        <h3>Initial Phase</h3>

        <p>In-depth analysis of Arcadis reports, focusing on the Sustainable Cities Index (work with data).</p>

        <h3>Middle Phase</h3>

        <p>Selection of specific cities, industries, and businesses for detailed studies; conducting interviews and secondary research.</p>

        <h3>Final Phase</h3>

        <p>Development of dynamic presentations, workshops, and online content to disseminate insights and recommendations.</p>

    </section>


    <section id="results">

        <h2>Detailed Description of the Project Results</h2>

        <p>The project aims for cities with stable economies, where businesses, residents, and planners collaborate for sustainability. It encourages students and young professionals to choose careers that match their values and help make cities worldwide more livable, greener, and economically prosperous.</p>

    </section>


    <section id="quality">
